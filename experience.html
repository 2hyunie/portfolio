Intern at the Fuels Program

During this internship, I took part in three different projects, where I co-authored in two of the white papers that are soon to be published (TBD). 3. PROJECTS



❋ Scrollytelling Article on Central Valley Drought and Almond Farming ❋

[Jan 2022 - May 2022]

I lead this project of creating a scrollytelling article on the Central Valley drought and the consequences on almond farming.
